About Enderiac

The name Enderiac belongs to an EDM producer and YouTuber from Canada. He started producing music in 2020 with his first song, Get Glitchy, which was made using the Chrome Music Lab out of all existing DAWs. He didn't grow in popularity that much, and most of his comments were from the same viewers all the time. Until he stopped caring so much about popularity and started focusing only on what he loves— music.
